About Us

Who Is Automist Labs?

Automist Labs is a boutique automation studio built for entrepreneurs, agencies, and growing businesses that are tired of doing everything manually. We sit at the intersection of technology and growth strategy.

Our founder is a certified n8n expert and GoHighLevel specialist who has designed and shipped automations for clients across e-commerce, SaaS, real estate, and marketing agencies. Every system we build is reliable, well-documented, and built to scale.

We believe automation should feel effortless — not overwhelming. Whether you need a quick integration or a full systems overhaul, we tailor every engagement to your specific workflow and goals.

🎯 Results over complexity — simple systems that actually work
🔒 Security-first — your data stays yours
📖 Full documentation — you own and understand everything we build
